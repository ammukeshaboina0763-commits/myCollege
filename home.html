About Our College

GDCW Begumpet in Hyderabad, established in 1971, is a government college for women affiliated with Osmania University. It offers UG and PG courses in Arts, Science, and Commerce, with modern facilities and extracurricular activities. The college is known for empowering women through quality education and career support.

History of GDCW Begumpet College

Government Degree College for Women, Begumpet was established in 1971 in Hyderabad to provide higher education exclusively for women. It focuses on empowering women from underprivileged backgrounds through arts, science, and commerce programs. Over the years, it has grown into a key institution for women’s education in the region.
